What is Lexis Guru?

Lexis Guru is a personalized program for people who want to learn a foreign language on their own.

Weather it is work, travel or just a goal to expand your horizons, it is not always easy to find time and money to take a language class or hire a tutor. But you can learn it on your own. And now it is only a matter of knowing where to start, what materials to use and how to practice what you've learnt. Here is where Lexis Guru comes in handy. The program is based on your personal goals, learning style, life routine and interests.

I AM
DINA O'CONNOR

Born and raised in Russia, I started learning foreign languages in middle school. At university my major was Linguistics and Translation and I have experience in learning such languages as: English, French, German, Latin and Chinese.

I moved to the United States in 2009 and continued to improve my language skills, I've met people from all around the world and realized that my life will always be connected with learning about different cultures and languages.
